ABOUT US

Welcome to Inquizitive!

Inquizitive is an interactive quiz platform designed to make learning joyous and engaging. Challenge yourself, improve your skills, and enjoy every step of the journey!

Our Mission

This platform provides a space where "USERS" can take quizzes, track their performance, upvote quizzes based on relevance, climb the leaderboard, enhance their knowledge and hone their quizzing skills. On the other hand, "ADMIN" can create quizzes using AI, control things like timing and scoring.

Our Vision

Inquizitive aims to be the go-to destination for quiz enthusiasts. A place where knowledge is shared, skills are tested, and learning never stops.
